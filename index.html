A Letter For You

Happy Birthday, Addy.


I am so grateful to God for the gift of your life and for bringing you into mine. Thank you for being endlessly patient with me, even in the moments when I know I can be a lot to handle. That kindness of yours never goes unnoticed.


My prayer for you is that the Lord will continue to do mighty and wonderful things in your life — that He will open doors you never imagined and take you to places beyond your wildest dreams. Above all, I pray that your love for Him continues to deepen and flourish as you grow into everything He has called you to be.


And I also pray that when the time is right, the Lord will bring you a husband after His own heart — a man who is intentional, serious about you, and worthy of the incredible woman you are. No funny business, no unserious men — only God's very best for you.


May the Lord bless you abundantly, today and always.

With love and gratitude
